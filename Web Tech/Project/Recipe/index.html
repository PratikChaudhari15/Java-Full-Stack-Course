Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Milk Powder
  • 1/4 cup All-purpose Flour (Maida)
  • 2 tbsp Ghee
  • 1/4 cup Milk (warm)
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  • Oil or Ghee (for deep frying)
  • 1.5 cups Sugar
  • 1.5 cups Water
  • 4-5 Cardamom Pods
  • Rose Water or Saffron (optional)

Recipe

  1. In a bowl, mix milk powder, all-purpose flour, baking soda, and ghee.
  2. Add warm milk little by little and knead into a soft dough. Let it rest for 10 minutes.
  3. In a pan, add sugar, water, cardamom, and a few saffron strands. Boil it for 5-6 minutes to make sugar syrup. Add rose water (optional).
  4. Make small smooth balls from the dough. Heat oil/ghee and fry them on low flame until golden brown.
  5. Soak the fried balls in warm sugar syrup for at least 1-2 hours.
  6. Serve warm or chilled. Enjoy your delicious homemade Gulab Jamun!

Barifi

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Milk Powder
  • 1 cup Condensed Milk
  • 1/4 cup Ghee
  • 1/2 tsp Cardamom Powder
  • Chopped Dry Fruits (optional)
  • Saffron Strands (optional)

Recipe

  1. Heat ghee in a non-stick pan and add milk powder to it.
  2. Add condensed milk and stir continuously to avoid lumps.
  3. Cook on low heat until the mixture thickens and leaves the sides of the pan.
  4. Add cardamom powder and mix well. You can also add saffron and dry fruits at this stage.
  5. Grease a plate with ghee and pour the mixture. Spread evenly and let it cool.
  6. Once set, cut into square or diamond-shaped pieces and serve your delicious Barfi!

Rasgulla

Ingredients

  • Chhena (cottage cheese)
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Cardamom

Recipe

  1. Knead chhena into a smooth dough.
  2. Make small balls and boil them in sugar syrup.
  3. Let them soak and cool down.
  4. Serve chilled with syrup.

Coconut Ladoo

Ingredients

  • Desiccated coconut
  • Condensed milk
  • Cardamom powder
  • Ghee

Recipe

  1. Roast coconut in ghee.
  2. Add condensed milk and mix well.
  3. Shape into balls while still warm.
  4. Roll in more coconut and serve.

Carrot Halwa (Gajar ka Halwa)

Ingredients

  • Grated carrots
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Ghee
  • Cardamom powder
  • Cashews and almonds (for garnish)

Recipe

  1. Cook grated carrots in milk until soft.
  2. Add sugar and cook until the mixture thickens.
  3. Fry cashews and almonds in ghee, then add to the mixture.
  4. Serve hot, garnished with more nuts.
